PanelVault
Vision

PanelVault is a professional iPhone application for electrical contractors, panel builders and maintenance companies.

The goal is to archive every electrical distribution board ever built, making it easy to find photos, drawings, manufacturers, components and project history years later.

The application should feel like a premium Apple application rather than an industrial tool.

Main Goals
Store every electrical project.
Store every distribution board.
Archive all photos.
Archive PDF drawings.
Track customers.
Track manufacturers.
Keep project history forever.
Make searching instant.
Replace folders full of photos and WhatsApp messages.
